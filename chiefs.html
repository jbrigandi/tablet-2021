National Football League

The National Football League is a professional American football league consisting of 32 teams, divided equally between the National Football Conference and the American Football Conference.

Kansas City Chiefs Overview

The Kansas City Chiefs are a professional American football team based in Kansas City, Missouri. The Chiefs compete in the National Football League (NFL) as a member club of the league's American Football Conference (AFC) West division. The Chiefs are located in Kansas City, Missouri, and play their home games at Arrowhead Stadium. The team began to play in 1960, and is owned by the Hunt family. The Chairman and CEO of the Chiefs is Clark Hunt, the President of the Chiefs is Mark Donovan, the Head Coach is Andy Reid, and the General Manager is Brett Veach. The Chiefs' colors are red, gold, and white.

Kansas City Chiefs History

The Chiefs were founded in 1960 as the Dallas Texans by businessman Lamar Hunt, and was a charter member of the American Football League (AFL). In spring 1963, the team relocated to Kansas City, and assumed its current name.[9][10] The Chiefs joined the NFL as a result of the merger in 1970, and the team is valued at over $2 billion. The Chiefs won three AFL championships, in 1962, 1966, and 1969, and were the second AFL team. The Chiefs won the AFC in 2019, and 2020, which granted them Super Bowl appearances. The Chiefs won the Super Bowl in 2019, but lost in the Super Bowl to Tom Brady and the Bucs in 2020.

Chiefs' Historical Leaders

The Chiefs' all time passsing leaders are: Len Dawson with 28,507 Passing Yards and 237 Passing Touchdowns. Trent Green with 21,459 Passing Yards and 118 Passing Touchdowns. Alex Smith with 17,608 Passing Yards and 102 Passing Touchdowns. The Kansas City Chiefs' all time rushing leaders are Jamaal Charles with 7,260 rushing yards, Priest Holmes with 6,070 rushing yards, and Larry Johnson with 6,015 rushing yards. The Chiefs' all time receiving leaders are Tony Gonzalez with 10,940 receiving yards, Travis Kelce with 8,441 receiving yards, and Dwayne Bowe with 7,155 receiving yards.

Cheifs' Current Leaders

For the 2021 Season, the current passing leader is Patrick Mahomes with 2,368 passing yards and 19 passing touchdowns, the current rushing leader is Clyde Edwards-Helaire with 304 rushing yards and 0 rushing touchdowns, and the receiving leader is Tyreek Hill with 735 receiving yards and 6 receiving touchdowns. On the defensive side of the ball, Nick Bolton is leading the team with 66 tackles, Chris Jones is leading the team in sacks with 3.0 sacks, and Willie Gay and Tyrann Mathieu are leading the team in interceptions with 2 interceptions each.
