National Football League

The National Football League is a professional American football league consisting of 32 teams, divided equally between the National Football Conference and the American Football Conference.

Las Vegas Raiders Overview

The Las Vegas Raiders are a professional American football team based in the Las Vegas metropolitan area. The Raiders compete in the National Football League (NFL) as a member club of the league's American Football Conference (AFC) West division. The club plays its home games at Allegiant Stadium in Paradise, Nevada, and is headquartered in Henderson, Nevada. The team was founded on January 30, 1960, and was originally based in Oakland, California, prior to their move to Las Vegas in 2017. The team's Co-Owners are Mark and Carol Davis, the team's interim President is Dan Ventrellle, the team's interim Head coach is Rich Bisaccia, and the team's General Manager is Mike Mayock. The teams colors are silver and black, and their mascot is the Raider Rusher.

Las Vegas Raiders History

Starting in 1965, the Raiders posted winning records 19 of the next 20 years. During that period, they won 12 divisional championships, the 1967 AFL championship, AFC championships in 1976, 1980 and 1983 and victories in Super Bowls XI, XV and XVIII. The Raiders are also the only team, NFL or AFL, to play in the Super Bowl in the 1960s, 1970s and 1980s. In the 30-year period dating back to when Davis took over in 1963 up through 1992, the Raiders' winning record of .661 with 285 victories, 146 losses and 11 ties ranks as the best among all major sports teams.

Raiders' Historical Leaders

The Raiders' all time passsing leaders are: Derek Carr with 29,165 Passing Yards and 182 Passing Touchdowns. Ken Stabler with 19,078 Passing Yards and 150 Passing Touchdowns. Rich Gannon with 17,585 Passing Yards and 114 Passing Touchdowns. From a rushing perspective, the Raiders' all time rushing leaders are Marcus Allen with 8,545 rushing yards, Mark Van Eeghen with 5,907 rushing yards, and Clem Daniels with 5,103 rushing yards. The Las Vegas Raiders' all time receiving leaders are Tim Brown with 14,734 receiving yards, and 99 receiving touchdowns, Fred Biletnikoff with 8,974 receiving yards and 76 receiving touchdowns, and Cliff Branch with 8,685 receiving yards and 67 receiving touchdowns.

Raiders' Current Leaders

For the 2021 Season, the current passing leader is Derek Carr with 2,269 passing yards and 12 passing touchdowns. The current rushing leader is Josh Jacobs with 204 rushing yards and 5 rushing touchdowns. The receiving leader is Henry Ruggs with 469 receiving yards and 2 receiving touchdowns. On the defensive side of the ball, Denzel Perryman, a former charger linebacker, is leading the team with 81 tackles, Maxx Crosby leads the team in sacks with 5.0 sacks, and Johnathan Abram is leading the team in interceptions with only 1 interception.
